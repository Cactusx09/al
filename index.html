Facts03

What is AiTrading?

AI Trading strives to leverage machine learning and artificial intelligence techniques and makes them serve humanity in massive access to the trading technologies. The number of professional users of ML&AI-based trading is constantly growing. We think that this intelligent, trusted way of doing trading has to belong to any trader not just hedge funds and investment banks.

How does it work

Applying machine learning to trading techniques is not a brand-new approach among profi traders. However, we strive to make it easier for professional traders and even beginners. Our ML-based algorithms process and analyze huge amounts of data, and search for trading patterns with required success ratio and significant probabilities. During processing big data, AI Trading generates notifications when to trade and what to do.

Which problem is solved?

There are widely known problems every trader face during trading. You can tell right when you look at any trader’s working place. Lots of monitors, overwhelming bulks of information, stressful decisions they need to make every day, unhealthy environment. Those are just a few of issues we can diminish, or even eliminate. AI Trading allows you to choose trading patterns most comfortable for you, and of course profitable. AI Trading processes big data and figures out when the situation is in time to make a trade into your pattern.
